  • Augustin Henri Rene Pineau du Terte ...(Henri will have his own section to detail his accomplishments)
    • Served in the French army and received a classical education in art adn music. Immigrated to the United States with his wife Marie. Designed and constructed jewelry for Charles Tiffany in New York City. Taught violin and other instruments. Updated a shorthand system taught in NJ high schools. Passed out from heat on a NY subway and was reported to have died (but hadn't). Cultivated grape vines and made his own wine. "Rennaisance Man". Designed his own photo enlarging mechanism.
